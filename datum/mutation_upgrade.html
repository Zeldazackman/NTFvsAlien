mutation_upgrade
/datum/mutation_upgrade

Vars

alertThe alert that was given to the owner.
alert_typepathThe typepath of the alert to be given.
categoryThe category slot that this upgrade takes. This prevents the owner from buying additional mutation that have the same category.
conflicting_mutation_typesIf the prospective xenomorph_owner already has one of these mutation types, they cannot get this mutation.
descThe description that is displayed in the TGUI.
nameThe name that is displayed in the TGUI.
required_structureThe structure that needs to exist for a successful purchase.
xenomorph_ownerThe xenomorph owner of this mutation upgrade.

Procs

DestroyRemoves the status effect for having the mutation, unregisters various signals, and then updates with zero structures.
NewSets up the owner, applies the alert for having the mutation, registers various signals, and then updates with current structure count.
get_desc_for_alertThe desc that the alert will have after updating.
get_name_for_alertThe name that the alert will have after updating.
get_total_structuresGets the total amount of structures for the mutation.
handle_alertUpdates the status effect alert's name and description.
on_mutation_disabledCalled whenever the mutation becomes disabled (going from non-zero structures to zero structures).
on_mutation_enabledCalled whenever the mutation becomes enabled (going from zero structures to non-zero structures).
on_structure_changeCalled whenever the mutation is created/deleted or when the amount of structures has changed.
on_structure_updateCalled whenever when the amount of structures has changed.
on_xenomorph_upgradeCalled whenever the xenomorph owner is upgraded (e.g. normal to primordial).
