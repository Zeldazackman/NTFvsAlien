Vars

minimum_health_rage_thresholdThe percentage of the owner's maximum health that the owner's current health must be under to activate the ability.
rage_plasmaDetermines the Plasma to remove when Rage ends.
rage_powerDetermines the power of Rage's many effects. Power scales inversely with the Ravager's HP. Ignoring calculation bonus, this can ranges from [0.25 at 50% health] and [0.5 at 0% health]. 0.5 and above triggers special effects.
rage_power_calculation_bonusThe percentage of the owner's maximum health to use to further increase / calculate rage power. Higher means more rage power which then means it is easier to reach super rage.
